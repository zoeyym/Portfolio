Roller Coaster Project

In my Physics Honors class, we were assigned to make a roller coaster out of paper at the end of our energy unit. We had to meet certain requirements such as having a loop-the-loop, two turns, a hill, and a funnel. I worked with one of my best friends to create this project and we worked very hard on it. We called it "The Dreamer" and constructed it with blue paper with hints of yellow. We also added white fluff to act as clouds. The entire project took us about half a week to create and we came across many difficulties we had to resolve. Unfortunately, I don't have a picture of the roller coaster we built but I have a slideshow we had to make at the end of the project.
